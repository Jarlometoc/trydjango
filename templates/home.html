Fibrillar Assembly Tool

FAT allows users to carry out a fibrillar assembly comparison of atomic models with fiber diffraction data. Users provide data in the form of a PDB file and in return receive a layline, Jmol and fibrillar score. The program takes a few minutes to run. Users provide an email to receive results.
